Create Challenge¶

Creates challenge space in Synapse

Input: Challenge Project name Output: The skeleton for two challenges site with initial wiki, four teams

(admin, participants, organizers, and preregistrants), and a challenge widget added on live site with a participant team associated with it.

For more information on challenges administration: https://docs.synapse.org/articles/challenge_administration.html

Example (run on bash)

>>> challengeutils createchallenge "Plouf Challenge"
